NEVER OUTSHINE THE MASTER

JUDGEMENT

Transgression of the Law

Always make those above you feel comfortably superior. In your to please and impress them, do not go too far in displaying your talents or might accomplish the opposite-inspire fear and insecurity. Make your masters appear more brilliant than they are and you will attain the heights of power.
